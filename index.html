About

A little about me. I'm a native Atlantan and hail from the University of Georgia where I hold dual degrees in Public Relations and Philosophy. My passion for programming came about through curiousity and self-study. I turned hobby into habit by enrolling in DigitalCrafts 16 week Immersive Bootcamp. Now, it's a lifestyle. When i'm not building, you can find me keeping up with my blog, checking out new hiking trails, counting down the days until the return of Westworld and couch-side coaching my favorite teams with friends and family.
